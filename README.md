# [**Brazilian-Ecommerce-SQL-Project**]

### End-to-End SQL & Portfolio Project

> **Transforming raw e-commerce data into executive-level business insights using SQL and Business Intelligence best practices.**

---

# 📖 Project Overview

Business Intelligence is more than writing SQL queries or creating dashboards—it's about turning data into decisions.

This project demonstrates the complete Business Intelligence lifecycle using the **Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset**. Starting from raw transactional data, the project progresses through data exploration, quality validation, business analysis, executive reporting, and dashboard design.

The analysis covers every major business domain of an e-commerce marketplace, including sales, customers, products, sellers, logistics, and customer experience, with the goal of helping executives make informed, data-driven decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The project aims to answer critical business questions such as:

* How is the marketplace performing?
* Where is revenue coming from?
* Which customers generate the most value?
* Which products and categories drive growth?
* How are sellers performing?
* How efficient are logistics operations?
* What factors influence customer satisfaction?
* What actions should executives prioritize?

# 📌 Business Insights

Some of the major insights generated by the project include:

* Revenue is concentrated in a relatively small number of product categories and geographic regions.
* Repeat customers contribute significantly more long-term value than one-time buyers.
* Delivery performance has a measurable impact on customer satisfaction.
* Seller performance varies considerably across the marketplace.
* Customer reviews are strongly influenced by logistics and product quality.
* Revenue concentration highlights opportunities for portfolio diversification.

---

# 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the project recommends:

* Expand investment in high-performing regions.
* Increase customer retention through loyalty initiatives.
* Improve delivery reliability to enhance customer satisfaction.
* Support underperforming sellers with operational improvements.
* Diversify the product portfolio to reduce concentration risk.
* Monitor executive performance indices continuously.
